Decision: GraphRAG vs Standard RAG

After working through this with Claude, I'm leaning toward a hybrid approach. Standard RAG for simple factual lookups, GraphRAG for questions that require understanding relationships between entities.

Key tradeoffs:

  • GraphRAG: Better for "how does X relate to Y?" queries
  • Standard RAG: Faster, simpler, cheaper for factual retrieval
  • Hybrid: Use graph for complex queries, vector for simple ones

— From Claude conversation, March 5
